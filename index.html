Partners and Affiliates

Cal ITE hosts regular technical events featuring local companies to bring opportunities to students. These events may also include office visits and field trips around the Bay Area.

Professional Events

Participate in resume workshops, talks/panels, and lunch meetings with professionals. Meet students from other universities in the Western ITE district during the annual meeting and network with industry leaders.

Transportation Team

Cal Transportation Team competes in the ASCE Mid-Pacific Transportation competition, planning and designing a solution to a given prompt. The team also runs a DeCal class so students can get academic credit for their work.
